Shinchan

Shinchan is a cherished Japanese manga and anime series that has captured the hearts of people worldwide. Created by Yoshito Usui, it follows the adventures of a mischievous and spirited youthful boy named Shinchan Nohara.

Doraemon

A cat-like robot of the 22nd century. Doraemon came to the present age to help Nobita. Doraemon has a four-dimensional pocket which holds a lot of gadgets.

Oggy and the Cockroaches

Oggy is a cat with a light blue body, green eyes, red nose, grey tummy and white feet. Oggy usually spends his time either watching TV or doing housework, when he's not chasing the cockroaches.
